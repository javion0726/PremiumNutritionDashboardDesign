Design a complete mobile fitness application called "Ascend."

Do not design a single screen.

Design the entire application as a premium design system with every screen connected through one consistent visual language.

The final product should feel polished enough to compete with WHOOP, Apple Fitness, Oura, Levels, Strava, and Nike Training Club while maintaining its own identity.

The brand is centered around:

Growth

Discipline

Consistency

Progress

Self-improvement

Confidence

Focus

The app should feel motivating without feeling aggressive.

Avoid generic bodybuilding aesthetics.

Avoid bright orange, purple, or neon colors.

The overall aesthetic should be elegant, modern, premium, and minimal.

OVERALL GOAL

When someone opens Ascend, they should immediately think:

"This feels like a premium app I would gladly pay for."

The experience should feel calm, polished, motivating, and world-class.

Do not copy existing fitness apps directly.

Create a unique identity centered around helping people become stronger, healthier, and more disciplined over time.
